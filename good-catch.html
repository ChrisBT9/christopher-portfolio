Good Catch!

In a world where you find yourself shipwrecked on a mysterious archipelago, you must fish monsters from the waters and capture them. Use these monsters to battle others monsters, gaining strength and skills until you are ready to face the mighty leviathan that holds you captive within the archipelago.

  • Duration Aug 2024 - In Progress

  • Team Size 9 People

  • Engine Unity

  • Role Lead Designer

  • Work In Progress

My Contributions

My primary role as a designer was focused on formatting and refining the majority of the game's UI elements, as well as contributing to the creation of UI designs. Additionally, I played a key part in organizing and placing assets where they belonged, ensuring proper formatting for newly created assets, especially regarding the overall profiles of the monsters.

Settings UI

Intentions

Settings Menu

I constructed the settings menu to address the basic accessibility needs of our game's users. My goal was to enable players to customize gameplay settings, including sound, graphics, and controls. Additionally, I included a help feature to guide players on how to play the game, in case they forgot certain mechanics. The UI effectively achieved these objectives.

What I Learned

During my time as a designer on the team, I learned a new approach to implementing UI in Unity (That being UI Toolkit), exploring both its functional complexity and usability from a designer's perspective. I also expanded my coding knowledge compared to when I first started. One valuable personal insight I gained was that I perform best under active supervision or while collaborating directly with others during work periods. Additionally, I developed considerable expertise in GitHub, reaching a level of proficiency where I feel confident teaching others how to navigate the desktop version.
