Unhappy Home Designer

You play as an intern to a job in Hell where you take care of the local residents by building their hell homes. The name of the game requests that you make there homes as unhappy as possible for your boss Satan to promote you to one of his next best employees! Play the level and try your best to get all three stars!

  • Duration Jan 2024 - May 2024

  • Team Size 7 People

  • Engine Unity

  • Role UI/UX Design

My Contributions

This was my first game where I decided that UI design was more my style and that my odd inclination for getting UI to feel right over other design elements pointed me on the direction of wanting to contribute to UI/UX design.

What I Learned

I discovered a passion for designing UI while working on Un Happy Home Designer. During the process, I learned more about UI and its implementation in Unity. I became more comfortable with the engine and achieved my design goals that I set out, learning a lot along the way about that process and what it takes to get there. I also expanded my experience in AI prompting, effectively using it to convey my ideas to other team members and improve our design process. Lastly I had gotten a lot more experience in Photoshop to tweak sprites and edit images for the UI elements.
