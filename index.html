Good Catch!

Game description

In a world where you find yourself shipwrecked on a mysterious archipelago, you must fish monsters from the waters and capture them. Use these monsters to battle others monsters, gaining strength and skills until you are ready to face the mighty leviathan that holds you captive within the archipelago.

Game description

A game where the player is a llama in a tank firing at other tanks going through levels with various difficulties. Defeat all the enemies and get through all the levels to beat the game! This game is inspired by Wii Tanks and the soul of Hell Divers 2! Spreading democracy with every projectile!

UnHappy Home Designer

Game description

You play as an intern to a job in Hell where you take care of the local residents by building their hell homes. The name of the game requests that you make there homes as unhappy as possible for your boss Satan to promote you to one of his next best employees! Play the level and try your best to get all three stars!
